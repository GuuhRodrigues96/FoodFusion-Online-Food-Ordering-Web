Allergen Legend

We use the following allergen codes in our menu:

  • W - Contains Wheat/Gluten
  • M - Contains Milk/Dairy
  • E - Contains Eggs
  • S - Contains Soy
  • N - Contains Nuts
  • Se - Contains Sesame
  • Mu - Contains Mustard

Important Note

While we make every effort to identify ingredients that may cause allergic reactions, there is always a risk of cross-contamination in our kitchen. We cannot guarantee that any item is completely free of allergens. Customers with severe allergies should exercise judgment when ordering.

Nutritional values are approximate and based on standard recipes. Actual values may vary depending on specific ingredients used and customization of your order.
